Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Register" button at the top right corner of the page, fill in your details, verify your email, and you're ready to start trading. The whole process takes less than 2 minutes.

We support over 150 cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Litecoin (LTC), Ripple (XRP), and many more. We regularly add new coins based on community demand and thorough evaluation.

Security is our top priority. We use bank-level security measures including 2FA, cold storage for 95% of funds, multi-signature technology, and regular security audits. We've never been hacked since our founding in 2017.

Our fee structure is among the most competitive in the industry. Maker fees start at 0.10% and taker fees at 0.20%. Fees decrease based on your 30-day trading volume, going as low as 0.02% for makers and 0.04% for takers at the highest tier.

Deposits are free and typically processed within minutes for cryptocurrencies. Fiat deposits via bank transfer may take 1-3 business days. Withdrawals are processed multiple times daily with industry-standard security checks to ensure the safety of your funds.
